## 图画作文

### 第一段

#### 全完了，幸好还剩点

总体描述

Recent years witnessed a growing recognition that inner qualities,beyond professional competence, play a decisive role in personal growth and long-term success.

具体描写

> 如果两个人在做一件事那就是
>
> In / At / By + 具体位置（人所在的位置）,
>  two + 人物 + are + doing（主要行为）,
>  with one + saying / remarking / commenting / responding that + 说话内容,while the other / another person + says / remarks / comments / responds that + 说话内容.

By the roadside, one man is standing beside a fallen bottle, saying that everything is ruined, while the other remarks that some still remains.

In the carton,two men are standing beside a bottle that has fallen to the ground,with liquid spilling out. One man looks distressed, covering his face and exclaiming that everything is ruined, while the other appears calm and optimistic, remarking that there is still some left.

评价这副画,具体体现了什么

> At first glance, the scene appears ordinary, yet it vividly reflects 

At first glance, the scene appears ordinary, yet it vividly reflects how different attitudes toward the same situation can lead to entirely different emotional responses.

What the cartoon subtly reveals is the lasting significance of maintaining a rational and optimistic mindset in the face of setbacks. 



#### 踢球，巨人

经常被困难压垮是因为经常放大困难，小看自己

The cartoon stages a devastating contrast.

> 这部漫画展示了一个毁灭性的对比

Two goalkeepers stand before the same goal, yet the challenge each imagines could hardly more different.

In one player's mind,  the goal looms impossibly wide, leaving him little hope defending it. To the other, the goalkeeper seems to occupy almost the entire space, rendering any attempt futile.

> 显得不可思议地宽阔

Yet at bottom both visions stem from the same psychological tendency: Human being are often defeated not by hardship itself, but by overestimating it.

## 应用文

At the end of day 总的来说

### 道歉信

**Suppose you have to cancel your travel plan and will not be able to visit Professor Smith.** 

**Write him an email to**：

1)apologize and explain the situation, and

2)suggest a future meeting. 

You should write about 100 words on the ANSWER SHEET. 

Do not use your own name. Use “Li Ming” instead. 

Do not write your address. (10 points)

注意语气措辞

第一段改写题干,换词，多几个同义词

I must apologize for change/withdrawing/postpone from my scheduled visit/meeting. 

The appointment was one I had long anticipated,yet a sudden family emergency now makes the trip impossible.

整段写完看过去式，三单等

第二段，讲具体因为什么事情

Earlier this week, news arrived that a close family member had suddenly fallen seriously ill. 

The situation has made my presence at home unavoidable,and in such circumstances proceeding with the trip would mean neglecting a responsibility that can not reasonably be set aside. 

For that reason, the visit must be regrettably postponed, so I hope another opportunity to meet can be arranged once the situation stabilizes.

第三段再一次的道歉

My sincere apologies again for any inconvenience that this change of plan may cause.

> I sincerely apologize again for any inconvenience **caused by** the change of plan.
